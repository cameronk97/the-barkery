Dog Biscuits

This homemade dog biscuit recipe is quick and easy to make. Oats are a great alternative carbohydrate for dogs that may be sensitive to wheat or other grains. Oats contain vitamin B, which helps maintain a healthy coat, and linoleic acid, which is a type of omega-6 fatty acid that helps keep dog's skin strong and healthy. Pure peanut butter provides nutrients and fiber as well as protein and healthy fats. It's important to ensure that none of your ingredients contain any added sweeteners such as xylitol, which is extremely toxic to dogs! Pure maple syrup is ok in moderation and is a good source of manganese, zinc and vitamin B2, which are vital nutrients for dogs. Eggs are an excellent source of protein, vitamins, minerals and fatty acids, which are great for your dog's coat!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (100g) oat flour
  • 1/4 cup (40g) oats
  • 3 tbsp (45g) peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup water
