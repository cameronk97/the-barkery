Dog Biscuits

This homemade dog biscuits recipe is quick and easy to make. Oats are a great alternative carbohydrate for dogs that may be sensitive to other grains. Pure peanut butter provides essential nutrients, fibre, protein and healthy fats. It's important to ensure that none of your ingredients contain any added sweeteners such as xylitol, which is extremely toxic to dogs! Pure maple syrup is ok in moderation and is a good source of manganese, zinc and vitamin B2, which are vital nutrients for dogs. Eggs are an excellent source of protein, vitamins, minerals and fatty acids, which are good for your dog's coat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (100g) oat flour
  • 1/4 cup (40g) oats
  • 3 tbsp (45g) peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp (15ml) maple syrup
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup water
