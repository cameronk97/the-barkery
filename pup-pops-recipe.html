Pup Pops

This recipe is great for cooling your furry friends down on sunny days. Greek yoghurt is a rich source of calcium and probiotic benefits. Bananas are low-calorie and packed with beneficial vitamins and minerals for dogs. Blueberries contain antioxidants, fibre and vitamins C and K, which help to support your dog's immune system.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup (236g) plain greek yoghurt
  • 1 cup (150g) blueberries
  • 1 carrot (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the carrot into edible sticks for your pup pops, or omit this step.
  2. Add the banana, plain greek yoghurt and blueberries to a blender and blend until smooth.
  3. Pour your blended mixture into moulds and add your carrots as sticks, if using.
  4. Leave your pup pops to freeze for at least 4 hours, or until solid.
  5. Serve to your furry friends while supervised.
