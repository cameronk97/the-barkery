Pupcakes

This is a cupcake recipe specially made for dogs! These pupcakes are a great treat for any occasion. Oats contain vitamin B and linoleic acid, which are beneficial for a dog's skin and coat. Greek yoghurt is high in calcium and protein and can be beneficial for your dog's digestive system. Carrots are an excellent source of vitamin A, which supports eye health, and apples provide vitamins, potassium, and antioxidants.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (50g) oat flour
  • 1/4 cup (40g) oats
  • 8 tbsp (120g) peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp plain greek yoghurt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tsp coconut oil, for greasing

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180째C (350째F).
  2. Lightly grease 8 cupcake liners or moulds with coconut oil.
  3. To make the icing, add half of the peanut butter (60g) to the greek yoghurt and mix to combine. Leave this in the fridge to set for now.
  4. Add the banana to a mixing bowl and mash.
  5. Grate the carrot and apple and add them to the mashed banana.
  6. Add the remaining peanut butter, oat flour, oats and egg to your bowl and mix until evenly combined.
  7. Spoon about a tablespoon of the mixture into each of your cupcake liners.
  8. Bake for about 20 minutes at 180째C (350째F).
  9. Once baked, set the pupcakes aside to cool.
  10. Once cooled, retrieve your icing from the fridge and use it to decorate your pupcakes.
  11. Serve them to your four-legged friends!
